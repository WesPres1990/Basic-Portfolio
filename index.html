About Me

Hello! I am a graduate of the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Economics from the Terry College of Business, as well as a recent graduate of a full stack coding and web development course at Georgia Tech Professional Education in Savannah, GA.

At the moment, I consider myself proficient with HTML5, CSS, and Bootstrap and therefore comfortable with creating static web pages; if you tell me what needs to be created for a page and how it should look, I will in all likelihood be able to do it. Throughout the course, I became more experienced with the use of JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, API's, MVC, React, and Handlebars and I am also familiar with Node.js, NPM's, and MySQL and Mongo databases. In terms of version control, I have been using GitHub for everything I have done thus far.

For most of my life, I have used Windows operating systems, though I have also used a Mac on occasion and look forward to gaining further and more in-depth experience with it in the future as I continue to learn technologies and languages such as Ruby and Python.

Furthermore, I am skilled in customer service, research, writing, and economics, with extensive experience using Microsoft Office applications and social media, while also possessing natural analytical abilities and an intellectually curious mind. I certainly look forward to continued personal growth and development in this field.
